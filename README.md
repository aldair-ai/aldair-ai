## 🧠 About Me

Hey! I'm **Aldair Espinoza** — a Data Scientist and ML Engineer currently pursuing my **M.S. in Data Science at UC San Diego**, with a **MicroMaster in Statistics & Data Science from MITx** and a **B.S. in Computer Science** from Peru's National University of Engineering.

I have **5+ years of experience** building end-to-end ML & AI systems across **finance, retail, and marketplace** domains — turning data into real business impact at companies like **Credicorp (Credicorp Holding, $13.6B market cap)**, **AB InBev ($117.7B)**, and **Bayer AG**.

I've led ML teams, shipped GenAI products, automated audit pipelines with LLMs, built computer vision systems at scale, and optimized marketplace revenues with predictive models.

When I'm not training models, I'm **playing or building games** 🎮 — game dev is my creative outlet and a passion that keeps me sharp on algorithms and systems thinking.

> *"I don't just build models — I build solutions that businesses actually use."*
